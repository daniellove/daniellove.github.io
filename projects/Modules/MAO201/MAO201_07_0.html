    In this lesson you will:
  • listen to a short oral text containing familiar phrases and sentences
  • pronounce Māori words, phrases, and sentences
  • combine visual and verbal language to present information and/or ideas.
    This lesson will review what you have learned during this module.
    This lesson will take approximately one hour to complete.
    You will be assessed by how well you:
  • understand a short oral text containing familiar phrases and sentences
  • pronounce Māori words, phrases, and sentences
  • combine visual and verbal language to present information and/or ideas.

Review – your chance to SHOW WHAT YOU KNOW

This lesson gives you the chance to show your teacher what you have learned so far. It is okay to go back over any of the lessons you have done so far before starting this review.

PRESENTING

Now it’s time to record yourself giving the weather report that you constructed back in Lesson Four.

  • Make sure you have spent time practising before you do this.
  • Remember that you can record yourself using your smartphone or tablet or laptop.
  • Once you are satisfied with your recording you can either upload your recording to YouTube and provide the link to your teacher or upload the file to the dropbox.
