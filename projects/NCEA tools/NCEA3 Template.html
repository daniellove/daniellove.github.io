OVERVIEW

In this module you will focus on:
  • defining an area for historical research
  • planning this research
  • selecting and recording historcial evidence
  • recording details of the sources.
The following module is broken into:
  • Selecting texts
  • Think as you read
  • Responding to written texts
  • Responding to visual texts
  • Timeline and template example sheets
  • Example response assessment sheet
Expected time to complete work:

This module will take you about eight hours to complete.

You will need:
  • access to a library
  • a pen, pencil and ruler.
